U S

Men's cosmetics revolutionary

We are a a company with a mission to provide skin care to men. We believe cosmetics and skin care are essential to a healthy lifestyle. Cosmetics and beauty should not just be limited to women. Bold offers a variety of masculine products crafted to tackle the biggest problems men face today. We are on a mission to change the cosmetics industry.

A little something about our team.

Name: Steven Irwing

Position: CEO

Bio: Steve is the creator of Bold. While at a wedding he came up with the idea for the product, identifying a significant need not being met bu cosmetic providers. He dropped out of UWO and pursued his dream to change the industry.

Name: Katie Chan

Position: CFO

Bio: Katie is a financial wizard. Her and Steven have been close friends since highschool, so he knew he would come to her for help. She is currently leading the company's IPO.

Name: Irina Banny

Position: VP of R&D

Bio: Irina has a deep resume she has worked with companies such as Nivea and L'oreal. She was excited about the opportunity to change the men's makeup industry and hasn't regretted her move. She is current working on a product to combat acne.
